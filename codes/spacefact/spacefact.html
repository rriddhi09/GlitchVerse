Space Facts That Are Out Of This World!

Space Is Completely Silent

Despite what sci-fi movies show, in space no one can hear you laugh at your own jokes. There's no medium for sound waves to travel through, making it the perfect place for introverts!

Astronaut Ice Cream Is A Lie

That freeze-dried ice cream sold as "astronaut ice cream" has never actually been to space. NASA prefers not to send crumbly foods that could float into equipment. Sorry, childhood dreams!

Venus Has The Longest Day

A single day on Venus lasts longer than its entire year! At 243 Earth days, it's the ultimate "I'll do it tomorrow" excuse in the solar system.

The Moon Is Stealing From Us

The Moon is slowly drifting away from Earth at about 1.5 inches per year. It's the galaxy's slowest escape plan, but it's working!

Space Smell

Astronauts report that space smells like seared steak, hot metal, and welding fumes. Space: the final frontier of weird smells.

Saturn Could Float

Saturn is so light that if you had a bathtub big enough, it would float! It's basically the rubber ducky of the solar system.
