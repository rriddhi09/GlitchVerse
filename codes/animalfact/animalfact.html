Animal Facts That Will Make You Go Wild!

Octopus Party Tricks

Octopuses have three hearts, nine brains, and blue blood. They're basically the overachievers of the ocean who still find time to squeeze through any hole larger than their beak!

Sloth Bathroom Schedule

Sloths only poop once a week, and they can lose up to one-third of their body weight when they do. Talk about the ultimate weight loss program!

Flamingo Yoga Masters

Flamingos can only eat with their heads upside down, and they stand on one leg because it's actually less effort than standing on two. They're basically doing yoga their entire lives.

Wombat Poop Cubes

Wombats produce cube-shaped poop. This might be the only example in nature where someone literally poops bricks. They mark their territory with these poop cubes, which don't roll away!

Mantis Shrimp Punch

The mantis shrimp can punch with the force of a .22 caliber bullet, so fast it creates a flash of light and a shockwave that can break aquarium glass. Never challenge one to an arm-wrestling match!

Goat Pupils

Goats have rectangular pupils that allow them to see nearly 360 degrees around themselves. They're basically walking surveillance cameras with hooves.
