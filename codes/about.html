About GlitzVerse

GlitzVerse Entertainment Pvt. Ltd. is an interactive entertainment company founded in 2025. Our mission is to build a vibrant digital hub combining casual games, witty humor, and fascinating facts for the curious and fun-loving audience around the globe.

We are headquartered in Bengaluru, India, and built by a passionate team of developers, designers, and creators. At GlitzVerse, we believe entertainment should be engaging, educational, and inclusive — all while being wrapped in a colorful, fun design!

GlitzVerse continues to evolve, launching new mini-games, trivia, and knowledge tidbits every month. We invite everyone — kids, teens, and the young-at-heart — to explore our ever-expanding universe.
